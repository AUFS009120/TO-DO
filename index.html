TO-DO

  • Type you TO-Do and press enter to add to your list.
  • You can hide and unhide the TO-DO box by clicking on pencil icon.
  • Hover the mouse on your TO-DO to get delete option.
  • You can strike-through your TO-DO by clicking on it.
  • To remove strike-through again click on it.
  • You can also delete the default TO-DO.
